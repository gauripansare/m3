Create More Folders

Two other students have been assigned to help you with this project, John and Sara. You decide to break out the responsibilities between the three of you as follows:

John: Collect sponsor ads and design the brochure

Sara: Write the brochure and auction item descriptions, design the invitations

You: Obtain auction items, locate businesses in which to advertise, and communicate regularly with your supervisor

Use the ribbon to create two new folders in the Document folder: one for John named BFK_John and one for Sara named BFK_Sara. Once you create those folders, move the following files from BidsforKids folder into the respective folders:

John:

  • Banner_BFK.jpg
  • Goodies for Any Event_Ad_BFK.pdf
  • Sponsors_BFK.docx

Sara:

  • Detailed description of Auction Items_BFK.doc
